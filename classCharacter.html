Detailed Description

Character class.

This class contains all the data what an existing champion requires. Like the hp, the damage, and the attack cooldown.
With theese parameters, we made some functions, to modell a figth, where 2 characters are hitting each other, until one of them dies.
The one, who still stands after the fight, is the winner!
